# Conditional Statement

The conditional statement—also called the _branching control flow_—is one of MATLAB's four **flow-control statements**. It comes in two forms:

- [[Matlab Conditional Statement - if, else, elseif]]
- [[Matlab Conditional Statement - switch, case, otherwise]]
